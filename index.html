~about jujube

hi! i'm juju, aka jujub3, multi-disciplinary artist and designer. welcome to my website! jujub3.boo/ a personal space where i archive my projects. i craftet this website modular and expandable, much like other of my works. also this website reflects how my messy brain needs to compartmentalize things, keeping clutter away. 🙈

as a core value, i believe in shared experiences and knowledge through community. i see art as an open source meant to be shared, remixed, and built upon rather than a proprietary product to be bought and sold.

my work is focused on the intersection of sound, image, and design. i am passionate about creating immersive experiences that blur the boundaries between these mediums, inviting audiences to engage with art in a multi-sensory way.

from my involvement in the montreal rave scene, i am familiar with curating experiences where various mediums and people intersect. i have experience in music production, sound design, graphic design, web design, and interactive installations. my work is often characterized by a playful and experimental approach, drawing inspiration mainly from nature and the human experience.

WAVES — (web-design)

This website was created for an event series called WAVES which took place during the summer of 2021, and which synced with new and full moon cycles. WAVES was mainly a space created for people to harness the therapeutic benefits of sound in a setting specifically for curated for that.

This is a website that was created for WAVES, an event series that took place during the summer of 2021. WAVES offered a curated space where people could experience the therapeutic benefits of sound. The events were synchronized with new and full moon cycles, enhancing the transformative power of the sound-based encounters.

During the new moons, WAVES offered ambient shows accompanied by guided meditations. These experiences provided a sanctuary for relaxation and inner reflection.

On the nights of the full moons, WAVES transformed into a celebration of techno music and dancing. With pulsating rhythms and raw energy, attendees indulged in an exhilarating fusion of music and movement.
